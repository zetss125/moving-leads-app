🚛Moving Leads App: Logistics Intelligence Engine
Moving Leads App is a specialized Business Intelligence tool built for moving and logistics companies. It streamlines the process of identifying individuals and families who are in the active planning stages of a relocation, allowing moving companies to reach potential clients at the exact moment they need services.

🎯The Problem This Solves
In the moving industry, timing is everything. Most logistics companies rely on expensive, "stale" lead lists or complicated CRMs that take too much time to manage.

I built this app because I wanted a simple, accessible, and high-speed tool to gain quick insights into future customers without the overhead of enterprise software.

🌟Why Choose This App?
Zero System Friction: You don’t need to overhaul your current operation. This tool is designed to be a "Sidekick" to your existing business, easily integrating into any current workflow.

Rapid Insight: Skip the complex data science. This app takes raw engagement data and instantly filters out the noise, showing you exactly who is looking to move now.

Built to Scale: Whether you are a local "two-men-and-a-truck" operation or a national logistics fleet, the architecture is easily customizable and scales without increasing costs.

Vendor Independent: Stop paying big bucks for proprietary lead platforms. Own your data and your tools.

Main Functionalities
📦 Relocation Intent Filtering: Identifies specific keywords and engagement patterns that signal a high probability of an upcoming move.

🗺️ Geographic Lead Sorting: Visualize where your potential leads are located to optimize your truck routes and logistics planning.

⚡ Quick-View Dashboard: A simple, clean interface designed for busy dispatchers and owners to see the day’s top prospects at a glance.

📑 One-Click Lead Export: Easily move your filtered leads into your scheduling software or contact list.

🔧 Highly Customizable: Modify the filtering logic yourself to focus on specific move types (e.g., long-distance vs. local, residential vs. commercial).


🏗 Developer-First Architecture
Backend: Python / Flask — Fast, modular, and extremely easy to tweak.

Deployment: Fully optimized for Railway or Vercel for instant web access from the field.

Database Ready: Connects seamlessly to CSV, JSON, or SQL databases, making it compatible with almost any legacy system.

🚀 Future-Proofing
While this version is a robust Business Intelligence tool, its modular design is "AI-Ready." The way the data is structured here makes it the perfect foundation for future integration with predictive scoring models, similar to the architecture I used in my Retail AI Leads project.
